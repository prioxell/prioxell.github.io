Productivity Tips

Prioritize Your Tasks

Use the Eisenhower Matrix to determine what is urgent and important in your life.

Break Tasks into Smaller Steps

Dividing big projects into smaller, actionable steps helps reduce overwhelm and increases completion rates.

Practice the 2-Minute Rule

If a task takes less than two minutes, do it right away. This helps clear small items off your list quickly.
